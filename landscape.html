Concierge FAQ

Common Inquiries

Do you service historical estates in Great Falls and McLean?

Yes. We specialize in heritage properties and use non-invasive installation methods specifically designed to protect mature root systems and historic masonry.

What is the difference between standard LEDs and your 2700K lighting?

Standard LEDs often emit a cold, blueish light (3000K-5000K) that can feel clinical. We exclusively use 2700K warm-white fixtures that mimic the inviting glow of halogen or candlelight, perfect for residential warmth.

Does installation damage my existing landscaping?

No. We utilize a surgical installation method that carefully lifts sod and weaves wiring beneath the soil. Your lawn is restored to its pristine condition immediately after we finish.
