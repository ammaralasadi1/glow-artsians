Frequently Asked Questions

Are you fully licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Glow Artisans is a fully licensed business in Virginia and carries comprehensive general liability insurance to protect your property and our team, ensuring complete peace of mind.

What kind of lights and materials do you use?

We exclusively use premium, commercial-grade LED lights. Unlike store-bought lights, our products are more durable, shine brighter, and are far more energy-efficient and reliable for a flawless display.

What happens if a bulb burns out?

Our white-glove service includes proactive maintenance. While our lights are extremely reliable, if any issue arises, we will promptly send a technician to make repairs at no additional cost.

Can I see a mock-up on my home first?

Yes. Our design process includes a digital rendering of our proposed design on a photo of your home. This ensures you can visualize and approve the final look before we begin installation.

Will your installation methods cause any damage?

No. Protecting your home is our top priority. We use a variety of industry-best, non-damaging clips and attachment methods specifically chosen for your home's surfaces, never using staples or nails.

Do you handle takedown and storage?

Yes, our all-inclusive service includes complete post-season takedown. We carefully label, check, and store all materials in our secure facility, making re-installation next year even more efficient.
