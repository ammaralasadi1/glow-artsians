Our Passion for the Perfect Glow

A Message from Our Founder

My name is Ammar Alasadi, and for years, I've had the privilege of serving homeowners across Northern Virginia through my home service businesses. In that time, I’ve spoken with countless clients who, like me, believe a home isn't just a place to live—it's a canvas for your life's best moments, especially during the holidays.

I founded Glow Artisans because I saw a distinct need. Many of my clients expressed a desire for a holiday lighting service that went beyond simple installation. They wanted a partner in design—a company that shared their high standards and could translate their vision for the perfect holiday display into a breathtaking reality. They wanted a service that was as reliable and professional as the home they took so much pride in.

This is our passion: to provide that elevated, "artisan-level" experience. It’s the joy we find in meticulous design and flawless execution, and the deep satisfaction we get from seeing our clients' faces light up with the magic of the season.

- Ammar Alasadi
